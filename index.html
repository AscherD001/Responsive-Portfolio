About Me

An old soul, out of his own time. Remaining amongst the living, while his people reside beyond. Daniel is a caring person who loves technology and finds it difficult to interact with others in a meaningful way. A free thinker, he mostly keeps to himself.


Finding it difficult to obtain gainful employment in a career of his own choosing, Daniel has returned to academia. His intent, to seek knowledge and build a stellar portfolio with which to procure said position... and to elevate his career to new heights!
